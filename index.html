Frequently Asked Questions

How does NZT-48 work?

Welcome to 💊NZT-48 ($NZT), a BSC-powered token which aims to reward diamond hands, 3% fee is distributed randomly to one of the top 512 hodlers, 3% is burned forever, reducing the total supply. NZT is not your standard RFI token. The main difference between NZT-48 and RFI tokens is that insead of every holder only one top 512 holder randomly receives the 3% fee. What's wonderful about this mechanic is that to make you richer you have to be more active with the token circulation.

How the burning works?

An additional 3% of tokens are taken on each transaction; those are destroyed, reducing the total supply on each token transfer.

What does "burn" mean?

3% from each transaction is sent to a special address in the BSC-network. This address is completely inaccessible by anyone on the blockchain. Tokens sent to this address are considered "lost" or "burned" forever, effectively reducing the total supply.

Who are the 'addicts' and the 'junkies'?

The addicts are the NZT-48 holders. It is the community, the people who invest in the token. Junkies are the top 512 hodlers of NZT-48. All of them have a chance to receive 3% of transactions amount when the token is being purchased or sold by others. Bless them.

Why the top 512 holders?

We believe it to be more interesting this way. Picking an address between all the token holders would generate incentives to maintain hundred of wallets with the minimal amount of NZT-48 possible. Thus, the 512 limit generates more complex incentives, including incentives for making more transfers.

How is the random winner selected?

The address of the sender is used as the seed of a random number generator, and from that unique generator, the index of the winner is obtained. A series of mechanisms are in place to limit the ability of the sender to manipulate who is going to become the winner, making the token randomizing as fair as possible.
